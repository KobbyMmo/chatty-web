Why can't I connect to Chatty?
  • Restart your phone, or turn it off and on.
  • Update Chatty to the latest version available on Google Play.
  • Open your phone's Settings app > More > and turn Airplane mode on and off.
  • Open your phone's Settings app > Data usage > and make sure that Cellular data is turned on.
  • Open your phone's Settings app > Apps > Chatty > Data usage and make sure background data is not restricted.
  • Open your phone's Settings app > Wi-Fi and turn it off and back on.
  • Try connecting to different Wi-Fi hotspots.
  • Make sure Wi-Fi stays on during sleep mode.
  • Reboot your Wi-Fi Router.
  • Contact your mobile provider and make sure your APN settings are configured correctly.
  • Upgrade your Android OS to the latest version available for your device.
Is it free to send messages over Chatty?
Provided you have an adequate internet data plan for your smartphone and a current subscription in Chatty, it is free to send and receive messages and media over Chatty. Chatty Messenger uses your phone's internet connection to send and receive messages, pictures, audio, video, location, and contacts. As long as your phone is not roaming and you have not exceeded your data limit, messaging over Chatty should not cost you extra. If you are concerned about data usage, we recommend that you use a Wi-Fi connection, if possible.
How do I use group chat?
  • You may add up to 50 participants to any group you create.
  • You may create a maximum of 50 groups.
  • Only the group administrator can add people to the group chat. When you create a group, you are automatically added as the admin.
  • If you are the group admin and you leave the group, Chatty will randomly assign admin status to a remaining group participant. Only that person can add you back to the group.
  • You can always control your own participation in a group chat, by staying in or leaving the group when you wish. Remember that the group admin is the only participant with the ability to add people, so if you have any questions about being added to a group, or prefer not to be added, please speak to the group admin.
How do I add a friend?
Your friend's phone number must be in your phone's address book in order for you to start a chat with them in Chatty.
If you cannot see your friend in Chatty, please follow these steps:
  • Ensure that your friend has Chatty installed on their phone.
  • Double check that your friend's phone number is entered correctly in your phone's address book.
  • Enter the number the same as you would if you were to make a phone call to that person.
  • If this is an international phone number, do not use any exit codes or leading 0s.Start all international phone numbers with a + sign, followed by the country code. For specific examples and instructions, see this article.
  • Open Chatty and refresh the Favorites screen. On Android, this screen is titled Select contact.
Chatty communication between your phone and our server is encrypted?
