Schedules

Scheduling feature in chatty application allows users to schedule a meeting at a particular place. Which will then become reserved for the entire period the meeting is scheduled to take. It comes in handy for class representatives who move around securing an empty class room for students.

File sharing

The app allows for file sharing including pictures, audio and video files.

Personal and Group Notification

Chatty also alert users of unread or new messages from both group and individual private chats.
